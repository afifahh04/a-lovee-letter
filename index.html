A Small Pause Before Everything

Please read this without rushing, and find a comfortable place.

This is not just a letter.
It’s a small space I made before you read what truly matters.
Take a breath — there’s no rush here.

Before we go further…

Let’s start with something simple.

Tap the heart three times.

❤️

Okay. That was easy.
Thank you for staying.

One small question.

Answer honestly. There’s no right or wrong.

How do you feel right now?

One last thing.

Answer this honestly.

When things get hard, what would you choose?

This is the letter I wrote for you,
what I couldn’t say out loud.

Please read it slowly, take your time.

I wrote this by hand,
because some things feel more honest that way.

You can come back here after you’re done.

Before you go

Thank you for taking the time to read this.
I didn’t write it to ask for answers.
I wrote it to be honest, and to leave space for whatever comes next.

Whatever you feel after this is okay.

With love, always.
